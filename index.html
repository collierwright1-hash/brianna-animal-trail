How it works

A simple, joint-friendly movement game for a 9-year-old who loves animals. Five animal paths — Bear, Crab, Monkey, Flamingo and Cat — each a small family of real bodyweight moves. The first two moves of every path are open from day one (so there's always variety); moves 3–5 unlock as she masters the one before. Mastering a move takes a set number of separate days of practice — tapping "I practiced this" counts once per day, which rewards showing up regularly and keeps each day light and easy. Mastered moves chain together into Flows.

  • Every day (~5 min): the Daily Warm-Up — gentle wrist prep, a few slow breaths, and the week's featured move. Builds the streak.
  • Two short sessions a week (~15 min): a few different animals plus a flow. Quality over quantity.
  • Tuesday: her tumbling/gymnastics day — the app just suggests a quick warm-up beforehand.

Good form & safety

Every cue favors steady, controlled movement over pushing into big ranges. You'll see "soft elbows — don't lock," "stack your joints," and "press the floor away — don't sink" throughout. The holds (Bear, Crab) build control rather than just hanging on the joints, and there are no aggressive backbends. If a move ever hurts, skip it and pick another animal — variety is built in on purpose.

Save & back up progress

Progress is stored on this device. Use Export to save a backup file, or to move it to another device. The Google Sheet sync is the next add-on (see below).

Optional — Google Sheet tracking: this build can send each completed session to a Google Sheet via an Apps Script web-app URL (no IDE needed). The hook is already wired in the code (SHEET_WEBAPP_URL near the top of the script) — paste your deployed URL there to turn it on.
